WE HAVE SEATING SOLUTIONS

Welcome to Simple Seating Software! If you have seats, then we have solutions!

We build software for schools, restaurants, theatres, and any place that has SEATS. Using a pencil and paper is becoming a thing of the past, so why not turn your everyday seating chart into something that can be reused for many years to come. We can fully customize the layout of your establishment in our software and give you the capability to add and remove names and seating arrangements at your will. No more earasing or marker stains. Just point, click and drag....and there you have a simple seating solution!
