A LITTLE ABOUT OUR AGENCY

We believe amazing ideas come from everyone, everywhere. In fact, at Interior Minds and Designs, everyone within our agency walls is a designer in their own right. And there are a few principles we believe—and we believe everyone should believe in their own design craft. These truths drive us, motivate us, and ultimately help us redefine the power of interior design. We’re big believers in doing right by our neighbors. After all, we started in a garage and we believe this place has much to offer. So we do what we can to support the community we LOVE.

Over the past five years, we’ve provided more than $1 million in combined cash and pro bono support to Find A Way Home, an nonprofit organization that helps homeless teenages find a permanent home. Other community giving involvement throughout our agency history includes pro bono work for more than 10 organizations, direct giving, a scholarship program through the K. West College of Art & Design, board memberships, and ongoing participation in the Design Mind Club, which gives five percent of our company’s earnings back to the community each year.


AMAZING SPACES DESIGNED BY US

COZY STUDY ROOM

This interior design project was for a client who loves reading books and having a calm yet warming space to study and read. The client also hosted a weekly book club and needed an inviting space to have members share ideas on their book selection. Our paint guru Sam came up with the color scheme to make this room pop!

INDUSTRIAL KITCHEN

The industrial kitchen was an amazing design of combining sleek titanium appliances, grey cabinets with customizing lighting. The design creates open kitchen space for dinner parties and friday nights with friends. The combination of carpet and wood flooring will make your walkway stand out major!

UPSCALE DINING ROOM

This design came together for our client who wanted a luxary dining and lounging area for guest. We absolutely loved the spiral staircase so we picked furniture that would continue to increase the elegance of this room. All the furniture in this room was personally picked out by our expert designers.

BUSINESS COFFEE SHOP

We design for businesses as well! This was a coffeshop that wanted a setup within a major office building. We customized the space so that it was opening and welcoming to customers. It demonstrates an elegant yet blended look so it is a perfect fit for the surrounding offices.

COMFY HOTEL

Welcome to nature's hotel and we customized this room in a lodge located close to a scenery campground. Our clients requested the room to be as close to the surrounding nature so once again our paint guru Sam delivered and created a calming green space for tourists to relax and wake up refreshed.

HOLIDAY RESTAURANT

We loved designing and decorating this holiday and romantic atmosphere for our local restaurant client. This was the number one destination for holiday selfies. The wooden furniture and block hanging add elegance to the wooden wall structure.

STYLISH STUDIO

Do you have a studio or dorm that you need to make the most out of your living space and still keep it stylish? We customized this minimal living area with trendy colors and wall shelving. The wall containers keep household items organized to avoid cluttering in small living arrangements.

LUXARY LIVING ROOM

We design living rooms that you cant wait to get home and relax on the couch. This space is great for any and every occasion and the white furniture adds to the luxary yet stylish look. The lamp and portrait is hand picked by our expert designers and we can deliver that unique atmosphere for you as well.

ELEGANT KITCHEN

One look at this kitchen and you will keep coming back for more! This is one of our notable projects constucted by our team of expert designers.
